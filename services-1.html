What are the options ?

Freezone Company

  • - 100% Ownership
  • - 0% corporate and income tax
  • - Available for most activities
  • - Allowed to trade within the freezone and internationally. Trade within the UAE is possible through a local agent/distributor
  • - Exemption from all import and export duty
  • - Inexpensive set up fees
  • - Eligibility to residency Visa
  • - Easy and cost effective hiring process
  • - 100% repatriation of profits

Mainland Company

  • - 100% Ownership
  • - Benefit from UAE low tax system
  • - 0% income Tax
  • - Allowed to trade throughout the UAE and internationally
  • - Very wide options of business activities
  • - More option for office location within the UAE
  • - 100% repatriation of profits
  • - Eligibility to residency Visa
